# Title; Journal Authors Summary
1 Neural Correlates of Cactus Meditation: An In-Depth Imaging Study
Journal of Neurobiology and Meditation		 Kartika Lama, Kartika Sloth This groundbreaking study uses advanced imaging techniques to explore the impact of cactus meditation on brain activity. The findings reveal significant changes in neural pathways associated with resilience and tranquility, providing a scientific basis for the benefits of this unique meditation practice.
2 Behavioral Adaptations of Cactus Flies During Cactus Flower Season: A Comprehensive Study
Journal of Insect Behavior		 Tucano, Kartika Lama, et al. This paper presents a detailed behavioral study of cactus flies during the cactus flower season. The research uncovers fascinating adaptations that these insects exhibit, shedding light on their survival strategies in the harsh desert environment.
3 Unraveling the Mysteries of the Mind: A Cellular and Molecular Perspective
Neurobiology Advances		 Kartika Lama This seminal work delves into the intricate workings of the brain at a cellular and molecular level. The paper provides valuable insights into how practices like cactus meditation can influence brain function.
4 From Mountains to Microscopes: A Journey Through Neurobiology
Biographical Studies in Science		 Kartika Lama This autobiographical piece traces Kartika Lama's journey from the mountains of Peru to the forefront of neurobiology research. It offers a unique perspective on the interplay between personal experiences and scientific pursuits.
5 The Ocean and the Brain: Parallels in Complexity
Comparative Studies in Biology		 Kartika Narwhal, Kartika Lama This comparative study draws intriguing parallels between the complexity of the ocean and the human brain. It highlights how understanding one can provide insights into the other.
6 Spitting Contests and Neural Plasticity: An Unlikely Connection
Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience		 Kartika Lama This innovative research explores how participation in spitting contests can influence neural plasticity. The findings suggest that precision-based activities can have far-reaching effects on cognitive function.
7 Cacti, Consciousness, and Neurobiology: An Interdisciplinary Approach
Interdisciplinary Studies in Science		 Kartika Lama, et al. This paper presents an interdisciplinary approach to understanding consciousness, drawing on neurobiology, meditation practices, and the study of cacti. It underscores the value of diverse perspectives in unraveling the mysteries of the mind.
